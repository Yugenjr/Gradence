# Academic Tracking & Grade Loggers

Gradence provides robust utilities to compute your grading distributions, predict target scores, and manage attendance constraints offline.

---

## 1. CGPA & SGPA Calculator
* **Adding Course Grade logs**: Select your current active semester tab, add subjects, enter their respective credits weight (e.g. 3 or 4), and pick your grades.
* **Saving to History**: Once calculated, tap **"Save to Academic History"** and confirm. This archives the semester log and automatically increments your profile's active semester level.
* **Inconsistent Semester Levels Fix**: If your profile lists you as a Semester 2 student but you already have Semester 2 grades saved, the system synchronizes this by automatically advancing your profile to Semester 3.

---

## 2. Target GPA Predictor
* **Requirement Analysis**: Input your desired target graduation CGPA.
* **Forecast Reports**: The predictor automatically inspects your completed semesters history, calculates remaining credit windows, and outlines the exact SGPA you must maintain in upcoming semesters to meet your goal.

---

## 3. Attendance Tracker & Skipping Predictor
* **Setting Thresholds**: Enter course names and set your minimum required attendance percentage (typically 75%).
* **Logging Lectures**: Log class attendance by tapping **"+"** (Present) or **"-"** (Absent).
* **Predictive Forecasting**: The registry calculates whether you can safely skip the next upcoming lectures without falling below the threshold, or alerts you if attendance is critical.

---

## 4. Assessment Exams Calendar
* **Add Exams**: Register course exams with target dates and select priority levels:
  * `High Priority` (red indicators)
  * `Medium Priority` (amber indicators)
  * `Low Priority` (blue indicators)
* **Countdowns**: The home dashboard automatically parses scheduled exams and displays the remaining days counter.
